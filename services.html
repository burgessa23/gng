PROMOTIONS

Creating an effective promotional campaign that will resonate with your target market and generate a positive buzz for your store is not an easy task. Let Gossip & Glamour help you generate promotional strategies for your business including exclusive Pop-Shops, Social Media exclusives, Client Loyalty programs, Daily Deals and more.

RETAIL COPYRIGHTING

Gossip & Glamour can help you find the right words to sell your products. We specialize in copyrighting for marketing materials, product descriptions, website verbiage, customer newsletters and more.

EMAIL MARKETING

Constant Contact newsletters continue to be an effective way to get clients engaged in what’s happening at your store. Let us help you design a custom newsletter for your clients and develop incentives and strategies to get clients to sign up. Gossip & Glamour can also help create a content calendar for your business.

LOOK BOOKS

Creatively designed look books can help to visually showcase your store to customers. We can help you organize photo shoots that reinforce your branding and help construct digital look-books that effectively display seasonal key looks at your store. Or- we can help you create virtual look books using photos you already own.

FLOOR SETS/VISUAL MERCHANDISING

Whether it’s a seasonal floor set or visual displays designed to showcase new product, Gossip & Glamour can help you creatively design visual displays to sell your product and enhance your selling space. If you need an extra pair of hands to execute a floor move or need help with design and direction, we can help you get the job done.

SPECIAL EVENTS

Gossip & Glamour can help your business host special events including fashion shows, trunk shows, in-store parties and more. We have a wealth of experience (and industry connections) to make your next event a success!

TRAINING WORKSHOPS

The ability to sell product is the key to successful retailing. It's what sets apart companies that succeed and companies that fail. Gossip & Glamour can help train your sales force to close sales and develop lasting relationships with clients in 1-hour salesmanship workshop. We can also conduct seminars on personnel development, management training, niche marketing, and vendor relations.
