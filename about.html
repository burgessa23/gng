ABOUT SYDNEY CHAVEZ

Founder, Sydney Chavez is a true fashion insider with links to many social circles and community organizations in Seattle. Having spent over a decade working (and playing) in the fashion industry Sydney has her finger on the pulse of the style scene in the city. Her experience includes retail store management, training and development, visual merchandising, wardrobe styling, fashion show production for shows including Fashion First and Project Red Dress, plus and over six years serving as the Sourcing & Production Manager for the private label lines at Zumiez Inc. Sydney holds an Associate of Applied Arts and Bachelors of Science Degree in Fashion Merchandising & Retail Management and has worked for many fashion related companies including Benefit Cosmetics, HMS Host, Nordstrom, Fashion First, Juxtapose, and Styled.Seattle. Sydney currently holds the Regional Director position for Seattle’s chapter of Fashion Group International and serves as the Managing Partner for CRAVE Seattle. When she’s not working, Sydney enjoys indulgent trips to the spa, traveling, shopping, and hanging out with her family and friends on Queen Anne

ABOUT GOSSIP & GLAMOUR

Gossip & Glamour is a marketing and public relations firm for fashion industry clients in Seattle, Washington. Specializing in work with designers, boutiques, retailers and manufacturers, Gossip & Glamour can help companies reach their productivity goals when it comes to marketing and PR. Gossip & Glamour also works with clients on sourcing, production, manufacturing and wholesale projects offering experience and industry know-how to get the job done right. With a successful track record building solid relationships with clients and delivering results Gossip & Glamour is a trusted resource that is highly recommended by fashion industry peers. Contact Gossip & Glamour at sydney@gossip-glamour.com. For the latest fashion news and events, follow us on Twitter @GossipGlamour and LIKE us on Facebook
